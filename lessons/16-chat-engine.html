ChatEngine 在 QueryEngine 之上加多轮记忆。两种范式：condense_question（把对话历史压成一个独立问题再检索）和 context（每轮都检索并把片段注入上下文）。A ChatEngine adds multi-turn memory on top of a QueryEngine. Two paradigms: condense_question (condense the history into one standalone question, then retrieve) and context (retrieve every turn and inject the snippets into context).

🧩 生活类比🧩 Analogy
QueryEngine 像一次性问答台；ChatEngine 像记得前文的客服，能听懂“那它的退款呢？”里的“它”。
A QueryEngine is a one-shot help desk; a ChatEngine is a support agent who remembers the thread and understands what “it” refers to in “and its refund?”.

✅ 本课要点✅ Key points
  • ChatEngine = QueryEngine + 记忆，支持指代消解的多轮问答。ChatEngine = QueryEngine + memory, enabling coreference-aware multi-turn Q&A.
  • condense_question 重写问题；context 每轮注入检索片段。condense_question rewrites the question; context injects retrieved snippets each turn.
  • condense_plus_context 兼顾两者，通用性最好。condense_plus_context combines both and is the most general.
💡 设计亮点💡 Design insight
多轮 RAG 的真正难点是“指代消解 + 何时检索”。两种 mode 是两种解法——这也是为什么聊天不只是“给 QueryEngine 套个循环”。
The real challenge of multi-turn RAG is “coreference + when to retrieve.” The two modes are two answers — which is why chat is more than “wrap a loop around a QueryEngine.”
